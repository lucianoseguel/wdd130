History

The Club Mendoza Regatas was born from a shared dream more than 80 years ago, alongside the crystal-clear waters of Lake San Martín. Founded by a group of enthusiastic rowers and sailors, this club quickly became a symbol of adventure and community in the capital of Mendoza. From its humble beginnings, the Club Mendoza Regatas has witnessed generations of athletes who have challenged the waves and conquered the lake’s rapids. We have cultivated a passion for nature, tradition, and sporting excellence, becoming a meeting point for families, friends, and water lovers. Today, we continue to build on this foundation, offering unforgettable rafting and adventure experiences for tourists and locals, keeping alive the essence of Club Mendoza Regatas: connecting with Lake San Martín and sharing the excitement of adventure.
