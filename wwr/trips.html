Trips

Experiences

Dive into adventure with Regatas Rafting Club and discover the adrenaline of the Mendoza River. Our rafting experiences are designed for everyone, from those seeking their first adventure on rapids to the most experienced. Accompanied by certified guides, you’ll enjoy an unforgettable journey through spectacular landscapes, feeling the energy of the water as you navigate safely. Each of our trips offers the perfect combination of excitement, natural beauty, and fun, creating epic memories that will last a lifetime.

Explore our experiences

Classic Descent on the Mendoza River (Family Level)

This is our most popular experience, perfect for beginners and families. Navigate the moderate waters of the Río Mendoza in the Cacheuta area, surrounded by a spectacular mountain landscape. It’s a journey of approximately 12 km where you will familiarize yourself with rapids safely and enjoyably. It’s the ideal combination of adventure and natural beauty for the whole family.

Make a reservation

Adventure in the Atuel Canyon (Intermediate Level)

For those seeking a bit more excitement, this experience takes you to the impressive Atuel Canyon. You'll paddle between imposing rock formations and rapids of greater intensity. The journey in this place is about 16 km, offering an exciting challenge with unique geological landscapes. It’s ideal for adventurers who want to boost their adrenaline.

Make a reservation

Extreme Rafting in Potrerillos (Advanced Level)

If you are an expert or looking for maximum adrenaline, the upper section of the Río Mendoza in Potrerillos is your place. Here, the rapids are more powerful and the challenge is constant. You’ll enjoy a technical and action-packed descent with direct views of the Andes Mountains. An unforgettable trip for those who want to take rafting to the next level.

Make a reservation

Rafting with Sunset BBQ (Complete Experience)

Combine adventure with Mendocian tradition. After an exciting descent on the Río Mendoza, near Dique Potrerillos, we’ll be waiting for you with a genuine barbecue. Enjoy a relaxing afternoon of food and good times with stunning views. A perfect way to end a day full of excitement.

Make a reservation

Expedition on the Diamante River (Extended Adventure)

For the most adventurous, we offer an expedition on the Río Diamante in the San Rafael area. This experience is longer, with a 20 km journey that includes rapids of varying levels and semi-arid landscapes of great beauty. It’s an option for those who want to explore a different place and enjoy a full day of rafting in a spectacular environment.

Make a reservation
