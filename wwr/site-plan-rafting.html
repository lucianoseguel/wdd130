Overview

Purpose

Swimming is a great complete sport, some people choose to swim for health or to be an all-around sport. This site will help you find swimming routines, and be able to contact a local teacher and participate in live swimming classes in our clubs.

Audience

Doctors recommend swimming because it is a very complete sport, this sport is intended for

  • Children
  • Teenagers
  • Adults
  • People with Joint difficulties
  • People with hearth disease
  • People with breathing difficulties
  • Seniors
